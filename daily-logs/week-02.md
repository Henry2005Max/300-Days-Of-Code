## Day 10 - Febraury 15, 2026
**Project:** Review - Jest Tests for Day 1 Calculator
**Time Spent:**

### What I Built
- 45 unit tests for the Day 1 calculator

- Tests for all 6 operations (+, -, *, /, %, **)
